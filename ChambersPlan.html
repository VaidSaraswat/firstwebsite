Chambers Plan Overview

Who chooses the Chambers Plan?

Lots of businesses! There are no industry restrictions or minimum firm size. Whether you’re a one person operation or part of a larger firm, the Chambers Plan has a full suite of benefits for you to choose from.

Why do Businesses Choose the Chamber Plan?

Lots of reasons! The most common one that we hear…rate stability. The Chambers Plan can help you avoid drastic changes in your premiums from year to year. How, you ask? Well the pooled structure of the plan spreads claims over all 30,000 participating businesses. So, no one customer is ever singled out because of high claims.
